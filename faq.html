Resell Guide - FAQ

Supreme - FAQ

Q: What is the time of the release?
A: 11:00 am EST
Q: How do I know which items to go for?
A: Determine which items to go for by comparing an item's retail price with its presale price.
Q: What do I do if the Supreme site is lagging?
A: Keep refreshing the item category page until your item loads. You can refresh more efficiently using CTRL + R on Windows and Command + R on Mac. If you click the add to cart button, but the item does not add, keep spamming the add to cart button until the item adds. If you click the checkout now button, but your browser does not go to the checkout page, open this link into a new tab: https://www.supremenewyork.com/checkout. The link will take you directly to Supreme's checkout page. If the process payment button lags, spam-click it until it responds and processes your order. No! You will not buy more than one of your item if you spam-click the process payment button. Why? Because Supreme only allows you to buy one style per item. So Supreme will not allow you to buy more than one of your item.
Q: How long do I have to stay on the checkout screen before processing my order?
A: Three seconds.
Q: What platforms is Autofill available on?
A: You can install the extension on Chrome and FireFox.
Q: How long do I set my autofill delay?
A: Three seconds.

SNKRS - FAQ

Q: What platforms is the SNKRS app available on?
A: You can download the SNKRS app on iOS and Android.
Q: What size do I go for?
A: Size ten. If the size ten sells out, go for the size that is the closest to ten. So a size eight instead of size four or a size 11 instead of a size 13. The sizes with the most stock start at ten and work their way out.
